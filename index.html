Questions?

All frequently asked questions

Why did you start BOAS?

We believe profit should be used for good. Our founders have worked with companies who made billions, and NGO's who struggled to get enough money to save lives and the climate. We recognized that companies could have much more impact if they used their Profit for Good. So we started BOAS, where we save jeans, and donate 90% of your profit to the best lifesaving charities.

What do you mean with "Profit for Good"

Profit for Good (PFG) companies use the vast majority of their profits to make the world a better place for all, by using it to fund the solutions to massive problems like climate change and young lives lost to preventable diseases. Famous PFG companies are Patagonia and Bosch. At BOAS we specifically donate to save as many lives as possible, which makes our customers, employees and investors lifesavers.

Where do you donate your profits?

We donate to the best lifesaving charities like the Against Malaria Foundation. There are 5 million young lives lost each year to preventable diseases, because the people and organisations who save their lives don't have enough funding to save all these lives. BOAS' mission is to donate 90% of profits to save as many lives as we can.

Where do you get your vintage jeans from.

We buy most of our jeans from vintage wholesalers and we also receive donated clothes. The vintage wholesalers buy from sorting facilities, and the sorting facilities buy unsorted vintage clothing from the owners of the donation containers you find around Europe, where you can donate clothes you no longer like.
